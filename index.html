How to submit a correction or suggestion

I welcome thoughtful corrections (typos, factual fixes, citation updates) and constructive suggestions that improve clarity, accuracy, or usefulness. If your input is adopted in a revision or later edition, I will acknowledge it on this page.

What to include

  • Location: chapter/section + a short quote or page number (if print).
  • Issue type: typo, clarity, factual, citation, formatting, other.
  • Proposed fix: the corrected wording or a concrete recommendation.
  • Rationale: one sentence explaining why the change improves the text.

Preferred submission method

Email works best for most readers. If you share sources (links/DOIs), please include them inline.
